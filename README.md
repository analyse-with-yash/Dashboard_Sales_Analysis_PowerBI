# Dashboard_Sales_Analysis_PowerBI
Interactive sales dashboard analyzing revenue, profit, and regional performance. Identifies trends, top-performing areas, and growth opportunities. Built to support smarter, data-driven business decisions.

Sales Dashboard Analysis

This project showcases a Sales Dashboard designed to analyze business performance across key metrics such as sales, profit, orders, and regional trends. The dashboard transforms raw data into actionable insights for better decision-making.

 Key Metrics
Total Sales: 12M
Total Profit: 1.89M
Total Orders: 500
Total Quantity: 2K
Average Order Value: 24.95K
Profit Margin: ~6.5%

 Key Insights
Sales show strong fluctuations across months, with noticeable peaks during mid-year and year-end.
Kolkata, Delhi, Mumbai, and Chennai are the top contributors to both sales and profit.
East and North regions slightly outperform other regions in overall performance.
Online orders are higher than offline, indicating a growing preference for digital channels.
Profit margins remain relatively low despite high sales, suggesting scope for cost optimization.

 Features
Monthly trend analysis (Sales, Profit, Orders)
Regional and city-level performance breakdown
Channel-wise analysis (Online vs Offline)
Interactive filters for dynamic insights

Tools Used
Microsoft Excel (Dashboard & Visualization)
Data Cleaning & Transformation
KPI & Business Analysis

Project Objective

The goal of this dashboard is to:

Track business performance effectively
Identify high-performing regions and cities
Analyze customer purchasing patterns
Enable data-driven decision-making
